Why AI Resume Parsing?

Traditional resume screening is slow, manual, and prone to bias. IntelliHire transforms hiring into a data-driven, scalable workflow.

The problem

  • Recruiters manually read hundreds of unstructured resumes per role.
  • Inconsistent criteria introduce unconscious bias and missed talent.
  • Scanned or poorly formatted resumes often fall through the cracks.
  • Teams lack a unified, searchable candidate knowledge base.

The solution

  • AI-driven parsing standardises how information is extracted and stored.
  • NLP models understand skills, context, and domain-specific language.
  • Machine learning ranks candidates against job descriptions objectively.
  • ATS-style dashboards make your entire talent pool searchable and sortable.

Advantages & Limitations

AI accelerates hiring, but transparency about trade-offs still matters.

Advantages

  • Reduces manual screening effort for large candidate volumes.
  • Standardises decision criteria to minimise human bias.
  • Handles unstructured, noisy, or scanned resumes via OCR and NLP.
  • Improves time-to-hire and recruiter productivity.
  • Creates a structured, searchable candidate database.

Limitations

  • OCR quality degrades with very low-resolution or poorly scanned documents.
  • Soft skills, culture fit, and potential still require human judgment.
  • Domain adaptation is needed for highly specialised roles.
  • Models require continuous retraining as job markets evolve.
